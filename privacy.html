Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

FlickZZ Resources ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email, username, and (optionally) Google profile data when you sign up.
  • Usage Data: Downloads, ratings, and comments you submit.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device info (via Firebase).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the platform and its features.
  • To authenticate you and secure your account.
  • To display your activity (e.g. comments, ratings) to other users.
  • To communicate with you about important platform updates.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (by Google) for authentication, database, and file storage. Your data is processed according to Google's privacy practices.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security practices including secure authentication, encrypted storage, and strict access rules. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication sessions and theme preferences. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

8. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.

9. Children's Privacy

The platform is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

11. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us through our official channels.
